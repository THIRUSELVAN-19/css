The seasons of Japan

Japan has 4 seasons: spring, summer, fall and winter. In most years, spring is from March to May, summer from June to August, fall from September to November and winter from December to February. Along the Japanese island chain stretching north to south, climate differs from region to region, but the changes of season are distinct.
